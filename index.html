Distant Horizons

A mod that adds a Level of Detail System to Minecraft

This mod adds a Level Of Detail (LOD) system to Minecraft. This implementation renders simplified chunks outside the normal render distance allowing for an increased view distance without harming performance. In other words: this mod lets you see farther without turning your game into a slide show.
